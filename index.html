Behind All...

Suman Thapa

@jeigsaw

Hi, from the creator of this site that you are currently looking. I am a permanent resident of Jhapa, Nepal and am currently living in Kathmandu. I live off design and music. Plugging in my best musics while my fingers interplay the pixels in my computer, I could spend whole day long.
Fantasizing various objects, even characters and trying to provide life to them, I commenced this journey of graphic design from my childhood. It was just a thing that I could come on and on and instantly forget ever other thing. Later, it became an important epitome of my life.
I extended this journey when I stumbled upon web design and incorporated it into my what-i-do-most list. At first, I just prepared flat psd files of the design. But as soon as I got acquainted with HTML, CSS and JavaScript, I was impressed more than ever by their flexibility of going over just flat design. I could produce the whole prototype of the site with out complex backend languages. More important is "I am a pixel lover (designer) not a code geek (developer)."
Other thing that always pleases my heart is music (and I choose to believe its not just me :) Reaching the realm of music through my aural cavity was a sort of adventure I started from early childhood. Now I play guitar and my fingers, too nowadays can feel the aroma of music more significantly.
Scroll down to find out some of my accomplishment so far in the field that I am passionate about.

Thank you good people \m/
